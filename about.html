About Parrave Ventures

Leading the future of vending solutions across India

Our Story

Parrave Ventures Private Limited is a rapidly growing vending machine operator in India, dedicated to revolutionizing the way people access snacks and beverages in high-traffic locations. We specialize in installing and operating state-of-the-art vending machines across universities, metro stations, residential societies, and hotels.

Our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted partner for institutions and businesses looking to provide convenient refreshment solutions to their visitors, students, employees, and guests.

Meet Our Founder

Sachin Sharma

CEO & Founder

Sachin Sharma is the visionary founder and CEO of Parrave Ventures Private Limited. With a passion for innovation and a deep understanding of the vending industry, Sachin has led the company from its inception to becoming one of the most trusted vending machine operators in India.

Based in Zirakpur, Punjab, Sachin brings years of business experience and a commitment to excellence that drives every aspect of our operations. His leadership has been instrumental in establishing partnerships with prestigious institutions and expanding our presence across multiple cities.

Our Mission

To provide convenient, reliable, and high-quality vending solutions that enhance the daily experience of people across India. We are committed to delivering exceptional service, premium products, and innovative technology that meets the evolving needs of our customers and partners.

Our Vision

To become India's leading vending machine operator, known for our reliability, innovation, and customer-centric approach. We envision a future where our vending solutions are seamlessly integrated into the daily lives of millions, providing unmatched convenience and quality.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our growth story

2020

Company Founded

Parrave Ventures Private Limited was established with a vision to revolutionize vending solutions in India.

2021

First University Partnership

Successfully launched our first vending machines at Chitkara University, marking the beginning of our university partnerships.

2022

Metro Station Expansion

Expanded operations to Delhi Metro stations, bringing convenient refreshments to thousands of daily commuters.

2023

Hotel & Corporate Partnerships

Partnered with premium hotels like Radisson and expanded into corporate offices, diversifying our location portfolio.

2025

Continued Growth

Continuing our expansion with new partnerships and innovative vending solutions across India.
