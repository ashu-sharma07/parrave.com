Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about partnering with Parrave

How does the revenue sharing work?

We follow a transparent revenue-sharing model where you receive a fixed percentage of monthly sales. Payments are made on time with detailed reports showing all transactions.

Who handles maintenance and restocking?

Our team handles everything - regular restocking, preventive maintenance, technical support, and any customer issues. You don't have to worry about operations at all.

What are the space requirements?

A standard vending machine requires approximately 6-8 square feet of floor space with nearby electrical outlet. We can accommodate different machine sizes based on your available space.

How long does installation take?

Once we finalize the agreement, installation typically takes 3-5 business days. This includes machine setup, product loading, payment system configuration, and testing.

Can we customize the product selection?

Yes! We customize the product mix based on your location type and customer preferences. We regularly review sales data and adjust inventory to maximize satisfaction and sales.
