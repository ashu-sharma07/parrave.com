Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our vending solutions

How much does it cost to install a vending machine?

There's no cost to you! We handle all expenses including machine purchase, installation, and ongoing maintenance. We operate on a revenue-sharing model.

What locations are suitable for vending machines?

We install in universities, metro stations, corporate offices, hotels, residential societies, hospitals, and any high-traffic areas with good footfall.

How do I get support for machine issues?

We provide 24/7 technical support. Simply call our support number or use our mobile app to report any issues for immediate assistance.

What products do you stock in the machines?

We offer a curated selection of snacks, beverages, and refreshments based on your location's demographics and customer preferences.

How often are the machines restocked?

We monitor inventory levels remotely and restock machines based on demand, typically 2-3 times per week or as needed.

Can the machines be customized with our branding?

Yes! We offer custom vinyl wrapping and display customization to match your brand colors, logos, and location aesthetics.
